Game of Thrones War

You will have a random numnber at the start of the game.

There are four characters below. By clicking a character (Jon Snow, Daenrys Targaryen,
Lord Tyrian Lannister, or The Night King) you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number,
you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each character is hidden from you until you click on him/her.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each character.
